Basic Heading

The basic heading contains a may contain a kicker, title and subtitle or deck.

If the title length is less than 28 characters, no additional markup is required. If the title length is between 29 and 32 characters add ng-h2 to the h1 class, and if the title length is 33 characters or more add ng-h3 to the h1 class.

Example

Video: Media Spotlight

Misunderstood Microbes

Most Microbes Are Here to Help, Not Harm

<header id="article-heading" class="ng-article">
    <p class="ng-article-kicker">Video: Media Spotlight</p>
    <h2 class="ng-article-title ng-h2">Misunderstood Microbes</h2>
    <h2 class="ng-article-subtitle">Most Microbes Are Here to Help, Not Harm</h2>
</header>
Article Subheading Styles

The subheading styles can use any header tag (<h1> - <h6>). Producers should use the appropriate semantictag for the placement of the information

.ng-section-tease Section tease header
.ng-modal-title .ng-h3 Used for modal headings
.ng-promo-title-large Large Promo title
.ng-promo-title Small Promo Title
.ng-article-subhead Medium Promo title
.ng-article-subtitle Used in page title subheadings
.ng-article-lead Used in article taglines
.ng-article-kicker Used in page title kickers
.ng-article-series-promo Series Promo Tag
