Usage

The article component constists of the article itself, a title, meta data, an opening paragraph and dividers.

Class Description
.ng-article Add this class to define the Article component. Typically you would use an <article> element for this.
.ng-article-title Add this class to a heading to create an article title. Typically you would use a <h1> element for this.
.ng-article-meta Add this class to a paragraph which contains meta data about your article.
.ng-article-lead Add this class to the opening paragraph of your article to highlight it.
.ng-article-divider Add this class to a <hr> element to create a divider seperating different sections of your article.
Example

National

Agriculture loves company

by Cassandra Love. Wednesday, January 22, 2014

Agriculture is the art and science of cultivating the soil, growing crops and raising livestock. It includes the preparation of plant and animal products for people to use and their distribution to markets.

Agriculture provides most of the world’s food and fabrics. Cotton, wool, and leather are all agricultural products. Agriculture also provides wood for construction and paper products.

These products, as well as the agricultural methods used, may vary from one part of the world to another.
